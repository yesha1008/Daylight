Ugh

First of all: You have full permission to be mad. Or annoyed. Or whatever you’re feeling right now.

Whether it’s something small like a or something big like people not listening, or plans falling apart, you’re allowed to be pissed. You don’t need to shrink that. You don’t need to slap on a smile or say it’s fine when it’s very much not fine.

Frustration has a sneaky way of making you feel stuck, like nothing will ever work out and you’ll always be behind or misunderstood or one step from exploding. But that’s just the heat of the moment talking. Things will settle. You will find your footing again.

You’re not failing. You’re not being dramatic. You’re not weak for feeling overwhelmed. You’re doing your best with what you’ve got.

So be angry. Then breathe. Then come back to yourself gently.

You don’t have to fix everything today. Just make it through this wave. That’s already enough.

And remember, your beige mom is so proud of you even on the days when you’re stompy and frustrated and yelling at the universe! (Still love you. Even then. Maybe especially then.)

—A.
